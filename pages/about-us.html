About Us

Project Focus

The project aims to modify an articulated industrial robotic arm to perform FDM 3D printing, advancing innovation and sustainability.

Mission

Revolutionize the industrial usage of robotic arms for cost-effective and sustainable 3D printing solutions.

Sustainability Goals

The project supports Sustainable Development Goals such as:

  • Industry Innovation and Infrastructure (SDG 9)
  • Responsible Consumption and Production (SDG 12)

Startup Potential and Impact

This project has the potential to evolve into a startup, offering innovative 3D printing solutions that emphasize sustainability and affordability.

Future Plan

We aim to provide prototyping services and document our process through an engaging video showcasing the robotic arm's printing capability.
